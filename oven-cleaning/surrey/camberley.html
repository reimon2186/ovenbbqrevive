Our Oven Cleaning Process

Step 1: Booking & Coordination

Your oven refresh begins the moment you get in touch for a free, no-obligation quote based on your appliance and its condition. Once you are happy with the estimate, we arrange an appointment that suits your schedule. On the day, we give you a quick call before we arrive to keep everything running smoothly.

Step 2: Inspection and Preparation

When we arrive, our technician carries out a full inspection of your oven and removes all detachable parts such as racks, and doors. These are placed into our heated dip tank to break down heavy grease and burnt-on residue while we prepare the main oven for cleaning.

Step 3: Deep Cleaning

With the removable pieces soaking, we work through the oven interior. All internal surfaces are cleaned carefully to remove buildup and restore a fresher, cleaner appearance.

Step 4: Polishing & Reassembly

Once everything is cleaned, we rinse and polish the detached components and reassemble the oven carefully. We ensure everything fits correctly and looks well finished.

Step 5: Final Touches

To complete the service, we perform a final check and tidy the surrounding area. Your oven is left clean, refreshed and ready to use.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • How often should I have my oven professionally cleaned?

    We recommend scheduling a professional oven cleaning twice a year, depending on how much you use your oven. Regular cleaning not only maintains optimal performance and hygiene but also extends the lifespan of your oven.

  • Do you clean all kinds of ovens?

    Yes, we clean gas, electric, and combis. Each type requires special care, so we’ll explain what to expect from our cleaning service for each of them. This ensures that your specific oven is treated with the appropriate methods and products to achieve the best possible results. The only brand we don´t clean are AGA style ovens.

  • Can I use my oven right after the clean?

    Yes. Once the clean is finished and everything is reassembled, your oven is ready to use immediately. There is no waiting time required.
