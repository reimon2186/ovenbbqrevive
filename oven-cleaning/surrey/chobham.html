Our Oven Cleaning Process

Step 1: Booking & Coordination

Your service begins with a clear, no-obligation quote tailored to your oven type. When you approve the estimate, we arrange an appointment that suits your day. On arrival day, we call ahead to let you know we’re on the way.

Step 2: Inspection and Preparation

At your home, our technician carries out a full inspection and removes the sections that are designed to detach. These are placed in the heated dip tank to begin breaking down general buildup as we prepare the oven interior.

Step 3: Deep Cleaning

With the removable parts soaking, we focus on the oven interior. Each surface is cleaned methodically to remove residue and restore a cleaner, brighter finish.

Step 4: Polishing & Reassembly

After cleaning, the detached components are rinsed, polished and reinstalled carefully. We ensure everything fits correctly before completing the task.

Step 5: Final Touches

A final check is carried out, and the surrounding area is tidied. Your oven is left in a clean, ready-for-use condition.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • Do you clean all kinds of ovens?

    Yes, we clean gas, electric, and combis. Each type requires special care, so we’ll explain what to expect from our cleaning service for each of them. This ensures that your specific oven is treated with the appropriate methods and products to achieve the best possible results. The only brand we don´t clean are AGA style ovens.

  • How far in advance should I book?

    We can often arrange appointments quickly, but booking a week or two ahead gives you the widest choice of times. Short-notice bookings may also be available depending on the area. In the run up to Christmas we recommend booking earlier rather than later as we are very busy around that time.

  • How long does a typical clean take?

    Most ovens take around one to two hours, depending on size and how much residue has built up. Range ovens can take up to four hours.
