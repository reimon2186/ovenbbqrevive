Our Oven Cleaning Process

Step 1: Booking & Coordination

Your oven refresh begins with a free, no-obligation quote tailored to your appliance. Once you’re happy with the estimate, we schedule an appointment that fits your plans. On the day, we call ahead to confirm we’re on the way and keep everything running smoothly.

Step 2: Inspection and Preparation

When we arrive, our technician carries out a full inspection and removes the parts designed to detach. These are placed in our heated dip tank to begin breaking down residue while we prepare the main oven for the deep clean.

Step 3: Deep Cleaning

With the removable pieces soaking, we work through the oven interior. All internal surfaces are cleaned carefully to remove buildup and restore a fresher, cleaner appearance.

Step 4: Polishing & Reassembly

Once everything is cleaned, we rinse and polish the detached components and reassemble the oven carefully. We ensure everything fits correctly and looks well finished.

Step 5: Final Touches

To complete the service, we perform a final check and tidy the surrounding area. Your oven is left clean, refreshed and ready to use.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • How long does a typical clean take?

    Most ovens take around one to two hours, depending on size and how much residue has built up. Range ovens can take up to four hours.

  • Do I need to empty the kitchen before you arrive?

    No. As long as we can get to the oven easily, we handle the rest. We will also leave our working area clean and tidy after we are done.

  • Do you clean all kinds of ovens?

    Yes, we clean gas, electric, and combis. Each type requires special care, so we’ll explain what to expect from our cleaning service for each of them. This ensures that your specific oven is treated with the appropriate methods and products to achieve the best possible results. The only brand we don´t clean are AGA style ovens.
