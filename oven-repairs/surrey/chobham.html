Frequently Asked Questions

  • Is it worth repairing an older oven?

    In many cases, yes. Ovens are often replaced unnecessarily when a repair would restore proper function. If an oven is otherwise in good condition, a repair can extend its usable life and keep it working reliably. We assess the condition of the appliance honestly and will let you know whether repair is a sensible option based on the fault and overall condition.

  • What are the most common oven problems you fix?

    We repair a wide range of common oven faults that affect everyday use. These include ovens not heating properly, uneven temperatures, fans not working as they should, control panels not responding, damaged door seals, and issues that cause the oven to cut out during operation. Each repair begins with a proper assessment so the fault is addressed at its source rather than masked.

  • Are your oven repair parts and workmanship covered?

    Any replacement parts fitted during a repair come with a manufacturer’s warranty where applicable. While we do not offer an additional standalone repair guarantee, all work is carried out with care and professionalism. We also provide clear advice on how to look after your oven following the repair to help reduce the chance of the same issue returning.
