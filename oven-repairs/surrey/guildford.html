How It Works

  1. 1

    Contact Us & Describe the Fault

    Contact us by phone or message and explain what issues you are having with your oven. This helps ensure the visit is focused on the right issue from the start.

  2. 2

    Diagnosis & Clear Upfront Quote

    Once on site, the engineer carries out the necessary checks to identify the cause of the problem. We focus on diagnosing the fault accurately rather than guessing.   3. 3

    Fast Repair

    Test, Tidy, Aftercare

    After the repair is complete, the oven is tested to ensure it is operating correctly and safely. We leave the area tidy and talk you through what has been done. You will also receive straightforward aftercare advice and support should you have any questions once you start using the oven again.
