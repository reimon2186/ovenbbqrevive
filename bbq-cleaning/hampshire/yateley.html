Our BBQ Cleaning Process

Step 1: Booking & Coordination

Your BBQ cleaning service begins when you contact us for a free, no-obligation quote based on your BBQ and its condition. We confirm the details clearly so you know exactly what to expect from the service. Once agreed, we book an appointment that fits your schedule.

Step 2: Inspection and Preparation

On arrival, our technician carries out a full inspection of your BBQ to assess grease buildup and its overall layout. All detachable components such as grills, burners and trays are removed carefully. The surrounding area is protected so the clean is carried out safely and neatly.

Step 3: Deep Cleaning

Each surface inside the BBQ is cleaned to remove grease and residue. This includes sections often missed during regular maintenance. The aim is consistent cleanliness throughout.

Step 4: Polishing & Reassembly

Cleaned components are polished and reinstalled carefully. Everything is aligned and checked before finalising. The BBQ is restored to a tidy state.

Step 5: Final Touches

A final check is completed and the area is wiped down. The BBQ is left clean and presentable. It is ready for immediate use.

    We usually recommend professional BBQ cleaning once or twice a year, depending on how frequently it’s used. Regular cleaning helps maintain better hygiene, reduce flare-ups, and keep cooking performance consistent.
