Our BBQ Cleaning Process

Step 1: Booking & Coordination

Your BBQ clean starts with a tailored quote based on your equipment and level of use. Once you approve the quote, we arrange a convenient appointment time. Clear confirmation is provided so everything is straightforward.

Step 2: Inspection and Preparation

We inspect the BBQ thoroughly and remove parts that require deeper cleaning. Preparation ensures every area is accessible and safe to clean. The setup is organised to keep the process controlled.

Step 3: Deep Cleaning

We focus on the main body of the BBQ while removable parts are cleaned separately. Internal panels, lids and lower sections are cleaned methodically to remove grease, fat and carbon buildup. This helps restore both cleanliness and cooking performance.

Step 4: Polishing & Reassembly

Once all parts are fully cleaned, they are rinsed and polished thoroughly. Each component is carefully reassembled with attention to correct positioning and secure fitting. We check alignment to ensure the BBQ functions properly.

Step 5: Final Touches

We carry out a final inspection of the BBQ and wipe down nearby areas. The workspace is left clean and tidy once the job is complete. Your BBQ is returned ready for immediate use.
