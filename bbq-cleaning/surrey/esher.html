Our BBQ Cleaning Process

Step 1: Booking & Coordination

Your BBQ clean starts with a tailored quote based on your equipment and level of use. Once you approve the quote, we arrange a convenient appointment time. Clear confirmation is provided so everything is straightforward.

Step 2: Inspection and Preparation

We inspect the BBQ thoroughly and remove parts that require deeper cleaning. Preparation ensures every area is accessible and safe to clean. The setup is organised to keep the process controlled.

Step 3: Deep Cleaning

All internal and external surfaces are cleaned carefully to remove buildup. Attention is given to areas that affect heat distribution and flare-ups. This stage restores a fresher cooking environment.

Step 4: Polishing & Reassembly

Components are cleaned, polished and reinstalled securely. Each part is checked for correct fit before moving on. This ensures safe operation when the BBQ is used again.

Step 5: Final Touches

We inspect the BBQ once more and tidy the surrounding space. Everything is left orderly and clean. The BBQ is ready for use straight away.
