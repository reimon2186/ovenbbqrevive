Our BBQ Cleaning Process

Step 1: Booking & Coordination

Your BBQ clean starts with a tailored quote based on your equipment and level of use. Once you approve the quote, we arrange a convenient appointment time. Clear confirmation is provided so everything is straightforward.

Step 2: Inspection and Preparation

We inspect the BBQ thoroughly and remove parts that require deeper cleaning. Preparation ensures every area is accessible and safe to clean. The setup is organised to keep the process controlled.

Step 3: Deep Cleaning

With the removable parts soaking, we focus on the interior of the BBQ. This includes all internal surfaces. We safely remove grease, carbon build-up, and stubborn deposits to restore the BBQ’s efficiency and appearance.

Step 4: Polishing & Reassembly

Once everything is fully cleaned, we rinse and polish the components removed earlier and carefully reassemble your BBQ. We ensure each part is fitted securely and looks its best.

Step 5: Final Touches

To finish, we carry out a final check, wipe down the surrounding area, and leave your BBQ looking fresh, hygienic, and ready to use. Our goal is to give you a spotless result with no mess left behind.
