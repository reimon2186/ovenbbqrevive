Our BBQ Cleaning Process

Step 1: Easy Booking & Scheduling

Getting your BBQ professionally cleaned starts with a simple, hassle-free booking process. Contact us for a free, no-obligation quote based on your grill’s size and condition. Once you approve the estimate, we’ll arrange an appointment at a time that works best for you. On the day of service, we’ll send a quick confirmation call, ensuring a smooth and convenient experience from start to finish.

Step 2: Inspection & Setup

Upon arrival, our expert technicians carefully inspect your BBQ, identifying any areas that need extra attention, such as heavy grease buildup or rust spots. To protect your outdoor space, we lay down protective coverings on your patio or deck, ensuring a mess-free cleaning process while we work to restore your grill.

Step 3: Intensive Deep Cleaning

We begin by carefully removing all detachable components, including grill plates, racks, and heat shields, and placing them in our specialized heated dip tank inside our van. This powerful soaking process breaks down stubborn grease, carbon buildup, and food residues with ease. While the parts soak, we meticulously clean the BBQ’s interior and exterior using eco-friendly, non-toxic cleaning solutions, ensuring a thorough and effective deep clean.

Step 4: Precision Polishing & Reassembly

Once every component is spotless, we carefully reassemble your BBQ, making sure all parts are correctly aligned and securely fitted for optimal functionality. To further protect your grill, we apply a specialized polishing treatment that helps prevent rust and weather damage, leaving your BBQ looking sleek, refreshed, and ready to withstand the elements.

Step 5: Final Inspection & Cleanup

Before we finish, we conduct a detailed final inspection to ensure your BBQ meets our high standards of cleanliness and performance. Once satisfied, we tidy up our workspace, leaving your patio or outdoor area exactly as we found it—except now, you have a perfectly restored BBQ, ready for your next cookout.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • How often should I have my BBQ professionally cleaned?

    We recommend having your BBQ professionally cleaned at least twice a year, depending on how frequently you use it. Regular cleaning helps maintain your grill's performance, hygiene, and longevity. A deep clean before storing your BBQ for winter is particularly important to prevent rust and damage caused by grease and carbon buildup. This ensures your BBQ is in top condition and ready for use when the warmer months arrive.

  • What sets your BBQ cleaning service apart?

    Our team takes exceptional care in cleaning every part of your BBQ, including those hard-to-reach spots like the top surfaces and hidden corners. We use eco-friendly cleaning agents to ensure thorough results, and we finish by applying a special polishing treatment that prevents rust and weathering. This protective layer helps shield your BBQ from the elements, keeping it looking and performing like new for longer.

  • Do you clean all types of BBQs?

    Yes! We clean all types of BBQs, including gas, charcoal, electric, and pellet grills.
